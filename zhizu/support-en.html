How do I add an asset?

On the home screen, tap the ＋ button in the top right. Enter the item name, purchase price, date, and other details. Zhizu automatically calculates the daily cost once saved.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device (SwiftData). Nothing is uploaded to a server. Deleting the app removes local data — export a backup first (see below).

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Data Management and choose export. You can save your data as a file for backup or migration.

How do I change the language?

Zhizu follows your system language. In iOS Settings → General → Language & Region, switch to 简体中文 or English, then reopen the app.
