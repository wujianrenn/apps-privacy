Privacy Policy — 值物 (Zhizu)

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Thank you for using 值物 (Zhizu) ("the App"). Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains, plainly and accurately, how the App handles your information.

Summary

The App collects no personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device. There are no analytics, no advertising, no third‑party SDKs, no tracking, and no user account.

What data the App stores

The App lets you record your belongings, costs, statistics, and a wishlist. All of this content (asset names, prices, dates, categories, notes, wishlist items, theme and appearance preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device through the App and is never transmitted to us or to any third party.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect location, photos, contacts, or device identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics or crash‑reporting services.
  • We do not include advertising or third‑party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not require an account or sign‑in.

iCloud sync (optional, future)

If a future version offers iCloud synchronization, it would use your own private iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. In that case your data is stored in your personal iCloud container, governed by Apple's privacy terms, and remains inaccessible to the developer. This is entirely optional and off by default.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone, because we never receive it in the first place.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Your control over your data

Because all data lives on your device, you are in full control. You can edit or delete any entry at any time, export your data, or delete the App to remove all of its data from your device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
